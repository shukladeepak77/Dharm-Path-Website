About Dharma Path USA Foundation

A non-profit organization dedicated to Sanatan Dharma awareness, Vedic services, and community outreach — serving families with devotion and reverence.

Our Mission

Dharma Path USA Foundation is a non-profit corporation dedicated to providing Sanatan/Vaidik awareness and teaching with a focus on the moral aspects of life — helping individuals and families live in alignment with dharmic values.

We provide religious and spiritual services including Vedic rituals, prayer gatherings, scripture teachings, and community outreach to promote spiritual growth, well-being and cultural awareness. Every service is rendered with authentic devotion and deep reverence for our Vedic tradition.

Our Community Service Activities

Dharma Path USA Foundation is committed to serving society through community programs and volunteer initiatives — rooted in the principle that true dharma is expressed through selfless service.

🧘

Spiritual Guidance Sessions

Open sessions where anyone can seek guidance on scriptures, personal dharma, or spiritual challenges — offered with care and devotion.

🎶

Bhajan, Satsang & Sundar Kand Events

Organizing community bhajan gatherings, satsang sessions, and Sundar Kand events to foster collective devotion and spiritual upliftment.

📚

Teaching Children — Sanatan Dharma & संस्कार

Nurturing the next generation through age-appropriate teachings on Sanatan Dharma, moral values, and the importance of संस्कार in daily life.

💛

Emotional & Spiritual Support

Standing with individuals and families during grief, illness, or difficult life transitions — offering compassionate spiritual support rooted in dharmic wisdom.

🪔

Festival Celebrations & Cultural Awareness

Organizing festivals and cultural programs to celebrate, preserve, and share our rich Vedic heritage with the broader American community.

🤝

Charity, Seva & Volunteer Activities

Community charity drives, volunteer programs, and seva initiatives — expressing dharma through compassionate action and collective service to society.
