Community Seva

Selfless service is the heart of Sanatan Dharma. At Dharma Path USA Foundation, every program and gathering is rooted in devotion, compassion and a genuine desire to serve — for every family and every community.

Our Commitment

What We Do for the Community

As a non-profit foundation, our programs are rooted in the principle that true dharma is expressed through compassionate, selfless action and genuine community service.

🧘

Spiritual Guidance Sessions

We offer open guidance sessions where anyone — regardless of background or faith — can seek clarity on their spiritual path, understanding of scriptures, or guidance through personal life challenges — offered with genuine care and devotion.

Topics covered include: understanding dharma and karma, guidance through personal difficulties, scripture interpretation, and finding purpose through a dharmic way of life.

Request a Session

🎶

Bhajan, Satsang & Sundar Kand Events

We organize community bhajan gatherings, satsang sessions, and Sundar Kand Path events that bring families and seekers together in collective devotion. These are joyful, uplifting gatherings open to all — rooted in the living spirit of Sanatan Dharma.

Events include: Sundar Kand Path, Ram Charit Manas recitation, Bhajan Sandhya, Mata Ki Chowki, Naam Jap sessions, and community satsang. Available in-person and online via Zoom

Join an Event

📚

Teaching Children — Sanatan Dharma & संस्कार

The next generation is the future of our tradition. We nurture children through age-appropriate teachings on Sanatan Dharma, moral values, and the importance of संस्कार (samskara) in everyday life. Our goal is to help children grow up with a strong dharmic foundation — grounded in compassion, truthfulness, and respect.

Programs include: interactive dharma sessions for children, storytelling from Ramayan and Mahabharat, introduction to shlokas and their meanings, and values-based discussions on dharma, karma, and seva.

Get Involved

🪔

Festival Celebrations & Cultural Awareness

Our festivals are more than celebrations — they are opportunities to preserve, share, and transmit our rich Vedic heritage to the wider American community. We organize culturally meaningful programs around major Hindu festivals and cultural occasions, welcoming people of all backgrounds to participate and learn.

Programs include: Diwali, Navratri, Janmashtami, Holi, Ram Navami, and other Vedic festival celebrations — with cultural education, community participation, and outreach to the broader American public.

Join a Celebration

🤝

Charity, Seva & Volunteer Activities

Dharma is not only practiced in temples and homes — it is lived through action in the world. We organize community charity drives, volunteer programs, and seva initiatives that express dharma through compassionate action, giving back to society in the truest spirit of Sanatan Dharma.

Initiatives include: food and clothing drives, community volunteer programs, support for underserved families, and collaborative seva with local organizations — open to all who wish to contribute.

Join Us in Seva

Whether you want to attend a program, volunteer, or simply connect — we welcome you. Every act of seva strengthens our community and honours our dharmic tradition.
