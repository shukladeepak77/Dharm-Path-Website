Sacred Texts & Devotional Practices

📜

Shree Ram Charit Manas

Study of Tulsidas's Avadhi masterpiece across all Kandas — Chaupais, Dohas, Chhands and Sorthas explored with translation, meaning and commentary.

🌸

Bhagwad Gita

Chapter-wise exploration of karma, dharma, bhakti and jnana — the eternal dialogue between Arjuna and Krishna, applied to modern life.

✨

Ashtavakra Gita

The timeless dialogue between Sage Ashtavakra and King Janaka — rooted in Adwait (non-duality) and offering a direct, uncompromising path to self-knowledge and liberation. For sincere seekers.

🔔

Upanishads

Selected study of key Upanishads — Isha, Kena, Mandukya, Katha — exploring Brahman, Atman, consciousness, and the mahavakyas. Sessions include Sanskrit shloka reading with meaning.

🙏

Naam Jap

Guided group and individual mantra jap sessions — Ram Nam, Om Namah Shivaya, Maha Mrityunjaya, and Gayatri Mantra. Includes proper uccharan (pronunciation) instruction and sadhana guidance.

🎶

Bhajans

Learn and sing devotional bhajans — Kabir dohe, Meera bhajans, and classical satsang songs. Sessions include harmonium accompaniment and group sankirtan for collective bliss.

Typical Schedule

When & How Often

Sessions are offered weekly and monthly. The exact schedule is shared with registered members via WhatsApp group and email.

Weekly Online (Zoom) — Every Saturday, 9:00 AM CST
Rotating topics each week, 60–90 min per session.
Monthly In-Person (DFW) — First Sunday of each month
Location rotates across the DFW area. Address shared 3 days prior.
Special Deep-Dive Sessions — By demand
Multi-week focused series on a single text — Gita chapter, Kanda, or Upanishad. Announced via WhatsApp.
1-on-1 Mentoring — Available by appointment
Private Zoom session for personal questions, sadhana guidance, or spiritual path advice.

Why These Sessions?

  • Deepen your understanding of scriptures in plain language
  • Build a consistent personal sadhana practice
  • Connect with a like-minded DFW spiritual community
  • Learn correct mantra uccharan and meaning
  • Bring peace, clarity, and dharmic values into daily life
  • Open to all — beginners to advanced seekers

Session Format

Each online session follows a simple flow:

  1. Opening prayer & invocation (5 min)
  2. Text reading with Hindi/English meaning (40 min)
  3. Commentary & life application (20 min)
  4. Open Q&A (15 min)
  5. Closing Naam Jap or Bhajan (10 min)

Frequently Asked

Who can attend?

Anyone with an open heart and genuine interest in Sanatan Dharma is welcome — regardless of background, age or prior knowledge.

Do I need prior knowledge?

No — all sessions are designed to be welcoming to complete beginners. Seekers with background knowledge are equally welcome.

What platform for online sessions?

Zoom. The meeting link is shared via WhatsApp group and email before each session. No account needed to join.

Can I join from outside DFW?

Yes — online Zoom sessions are open to anyone worldwide. In-person sessions are DFW-area only.

🕉️ All are welcome. These sessions are offered in a spirit of seva — to share the light of our scriptures with whoever wishes to receive it.
