About Jyotish

What is Vedic Jyotish?

Jyotish (ज्योतिष) is the ancient Vedic system of astrology rooted in the Vedangas, the limbs of the Vedas. Unlike Western astrology, Vedic Jyotish uses the sidereal zodiac, which tracks the actual position of planets against the fixed stars.

A Jyotish consultation looks at your Janma Kundli (birth chart) to understand the karmic patterns of your life, the timing of events through planetary periods (dashas), and the remedies that can restore balance and harmony.
