As a non-profit foundation we go beyond ceremonies — offering community programs, cultural outreach and volunteer seva that bring people together in the true spirit of Sanatan Dharma.

🧘

Spiritual Guidance

Open guidance sessions for those seeking clarity on their spiritual path, scriptures or life challenges — offered with care and devotion to all who seek it.

🎶

Bhajan, Satsang & Sundar Kand

Community charity drives, volunteer programs, and seva initiatives — giving back in the true spirit of dharma, compassion, and collective well-being.

Seeking Seva

How to Request a Seva

Requesting a seva is simple — share your details and we will coordinate everything with full care and devotion.

Step 1

Choose Your Seva

Select the type of seva — Vedic ritual, path, bhakti gathering, Jyotish guidance, or a spiritual session.

Step 2

Share Date & Location

Let us know your preferred date, time, and whether you need in-person or online seva.

Step 3

Submit Your Request

Share your contact details and any special intentions or notes for the seva.

Step 4

We Coordinate

Our team reaches out to confirm, guide you on samagri, and ensure everything is prepared with reverence.
